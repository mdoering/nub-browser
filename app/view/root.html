GBIF Backbone

This is a special browser for the GBIF backbone taxonomy.

It aims to assist a user in reviewing the changes from the new backbone version to the current live one by providing comprehensive details for a taxon. It also visualizes the impact of the changed taxonomy on GBIF occurrences by offering a map view and various metrics.

Instead of showing numbers side by side colors are used to show significant changes with light red and red representing a decrease and light green and green an increase in numbers.

Only tested with Google Chrome! Other browsers are untested and may not work.
